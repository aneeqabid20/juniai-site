What Juni AI Does

Juni AI is an AI-powered kids learning app for iPhone. Parents create the account, set up child profiles, and children learn through adaptive lessons across multiple subjects in a child-safe experience.

Built for Parents

Parents are always the account holders. Children do not create accounts, cannot make purchases, and cannot access external links from child-facing screens.

AI Lessons

Adaptive lessons with MCQ, True/False, Fill in the Blank, and Short Answer formats.

Progress Tracking

Parents can follow mastery, lesson activity, stars, streaks, and weekly summaries.

Safe by Design

No ads, no child logins, no chat, no public social features, and no behavioral tracking.

Subjects Available

Maths (Grades 1-6) Reading (Grades 1-6) Science (Grades 1-6) Brain Puzzles (Grades 1-6) World Explorer (Grades 2-6) Coding Basics (Grades 3-6) AI Basics (Grades 5-6)
