Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Juni AI collects, uses, and protects information for parents and children.

1. Parent-Managed Accounts

Juni AI is designed for use by children under parental supervision. All accounts must be created and managed by a parent or legal guardian. Children are not permitted to create accounts independently.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Parent email address for account creation and communication
  • Child profile information, such as name, age, and learning progress
  • Limited usage data to improve functionality and learning experience

3. Children’s Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children without verifiable parental involvement. All information related to children is provided and managed by parents or legal guardians.

4. Parental Consent

By creating an account and using Juni AI, parents or legal guardians provide consent for the collection, use, and processing of their child’s information as described in this Privacy Policy.

5. How We Use Information

We use collected information solely to:

  • Provide and operate educational services
  • Personalize the learning experience
  • Improve application performance, functionality, and content

6. No Advertising or Tracking

Juni AI does not display advertisements and does not use personal data for advertising, profiling, or behavioral tracking purposes.

7. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share personal data with third parties for marketing or commercial purposes.

8. Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party services to operate and support Juni AI, including:

  • Supabase — secure backend infrastructure and data storage
  • RevenueCat — subscription and payment management

These service providers process data only as necessary to perform their functions and are expected to protect user data.

9. Data Security

We implement industry-standard technical and organizational measures to protect personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or misuse.

10. Data Retention

We retain personal data only for as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with applicable legal obligations.

11. Data Deletion

Parents or legal guardians may request deletion of their account or their child’s data at any time by contacting us. We will process such requests within a reasonable timeframe.

12. Your Rights

Parents or legal guardians have the right to access, update, correct, or request deletion of the information associated with their account or their child’s profile.

13. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page. Continued use of the service after changes indicates acceptance of the updated policy.
